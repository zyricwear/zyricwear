About ZyricWear

ZyricWear is an India-rooted fashion label crafting premium clothing for adult men and women, along with an exclusive line of ladies’ bags and purses. Our aesthetic blends Arabic-inspired elegance with contemporary street style — vivid hues, refined silhouettes, and quality-first construction.

We believe fashion should be expressive yet timeless. Every drop will focus on fabric integrity, sharp tailoring, and wearable art that elevates everyday style.

Founded by Osim Akhtar
Driven by passion for fashion, belief in quality, and a promise of customer satisfaction.
